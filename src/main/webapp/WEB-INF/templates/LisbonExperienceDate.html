Lisbon Experience Date Package

Find more about this wonderful Lisbon, a historical city full of stories to tell beyond every corner, where the sun shines 290 days a year.



There's no better way of getting to know the genuine side of Lisbon than going through the streets of Alfama and spend the day contemplating portuguese culture with your partner. This package will guide you through the daily lives of the citizens of this city and will provide you an experience that can be put only in one word: genuine.


This package includes:
Visit @ Tiles Museum

See a collection of some of the most beautiful azulejos in Portugal, and learn more about the country's traditional tilework.

After Dinner @ Graça Viewpoint

Watch Lisbon in one of best spots to apreciate its beauty and magic in the city's reference historical place.
